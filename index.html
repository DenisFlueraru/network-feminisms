▼ INFO //

This interactive interface is a technofeminist ecosystem inspired by Ivan Illich’s Tools for Conviviality and decentralized cyber-feminism. By turning away from the rigid, extractive infrastructures of corporate monopolies and digital enclosures, this project re-imagines the internet as an organic, living anarchy.

Here, digital tools are morphed into bio-mechanical organisms. They are not passive products to be consumed, but autonomous nodes. In a convivial network, power is never fixed, central, or hierarchical; it is a shared, shifting relation.

Every interaction you trigger ripples through the entire grid. By disturbing one organism, you alter the collective balance—proving that inside an organic infrastructure, we are all radically interdependent.
